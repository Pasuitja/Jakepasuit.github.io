What Makes A Ski Resort Good?

Each ski resort has something different than the other. Thats what makes each one unique from eachother. But there are universal things that a resort needs to have to make it good. The resort must be in a good location. Meaning good location for the customers and for snowfall. Like in michigan the resorts up north get much more snow than the resorts down south. This is because they get mucuh more lake effect snow. The resort must also have lots of different terrian. for example having a terrian park for the park riders to get their fun in. This is not all the reasons that make a resort good but the most important reason is lift line control. How long do you have to wait between runs? This is a huge problem for the ski resorts out west. The lift lines on a good busy day are unreal. I have had to wait over a hour in a line before which is something I will never do again.

Bad Things At a Ski Resort.

  • Over priced food
  • Long Lift Lines
  • Over the top ski patrol
  • Bad snow but the radar said otherwise
  • No parking
